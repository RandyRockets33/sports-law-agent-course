FAQ

Questions answered.

Do I need a law degree to enroll?

No. The course is practitioner-first and assumes no prior legal education. Lawyers, law students, agents, and career-switchers all use the same full-access certification track.

Is this self-paced?

Fully self-paced and asynchronous. Your progress and quiz scores sync to your account and survive across devices.

How long do I have access?

Lifetime. Once you enroll, every module and every library document is yours forever, including future content updates.

How is the certificate issued?

Sports Agent Academy Certificate of Completion is issued automatically when you pass mid-term, final, and capstone at 70%+. Each certificate has a unique credential ID and is verifiable.

Can professionals use it?

Yes. The course is built at practitioner depth while still being clear enough for serious first-time learners.

What if it doesn't fit my needs?

14-day refund, no questions. Email enrollments@sportsagentacademy.io within 14 days for a full refund.

Will content stay current?

Yes. Annual updates are included with every enrollment as NIL law, CBAs, and antitrust doctrine evolve.

What's the institutional / cohort option?

Law schools, athletic departments, agencies, and player associations can license a cohort version of the course or the entire library at academic depth. Contact us.
