About the academy

Sports Agent Academy is an online certification academy operated by Sports Marketing University. We were founded in 2026 on the belief that the standard agent-education content was theatre — surveys, fan-history, no working detail. We built the opposite.

Our philosophy

The standard sports law course tries to be everything: a fan introduction, a casebook survey, a career inspiration talk. Ours is one thing — a practitioner's operating manual for the modern sports agent and the lawyer who represents athletes. The course doesn't survey. It teaches you how to actually read an NFL Player Contract addendum, how to negotiate an exclusivity clause, how to comply with state athlete-agent statutes in three jurisdictions simultaneously, and how to respond when your client is named in a federal subpoena at 2 a.m.

Every module ends with a practical exercise. Every assessment is graded for working judgment, not memorization. The enrolled library — federal statutes, state NIL laws, league CBAs, NCAA Manual articles, full case opinions — is the same paper the working agent reads.
