Preface

Sports law is not a single doctrine. It is the working intersection of contract, labor, antitrust, agency, fiduciary duty, intellectual property, college regulation, discipline, and commercial leverage.

The agent's job is to understand where each system begins, where it ends, and where a client's career can be protected or damaged by a sentence buried in a form agreement.

How To Read This Volume

Each chapter is designed as a practice map: doctrine first, then the actors, then the document or dispute where the rule actually matters.
