Representing the Modern Athlete — Practitioner's Guide

Sample: the counsel-side operating manual and client-management frame.

The Modern Athlete As A Client

The modern athlete is not merely an employee or performer. The athlete is a media asset, brand platform, labor-market participant, investment vehicle, public figure, and often a young person under unusual pressure.

Good representation starts with a disciplined advisory structure: who speaks, who reviews, who signs, who protects privilege, and who tells the client the hard truth.

The Advisor Team

Agents, lawyers, accountants, financial advisors, brand managers, family members, team personnel, union representatives, and sponsors can all affect the same decision.

The practitioner must make the decision process legible before the crisis arrives.
